Common Questions

Mobile Welding FAQ

What does your mobile rig carry? +
Our truck is stocked for the most common welding jobs — MIG, stick, and cut-off equipment, common stock material, and the tools to handle most repairs on a single visit. If a job needs specialty material, we'll confirm that before we arrive.
Is there an extra charge for mobile service? +
We include travel within our normal service area in the estimate. For locations farther out, we'll be upfront about any additional cost before you commit to anything.
How fast can you get to us for an emergency? +
Depends on current workload, but we prioritize true emergencies — a broken security gate, a safety-critical railing, a door that won't close. Call us and we'll tell you honestly when we can be there.
Can you weld in tight spaces or indoors? +
Yes, with proper ventilation. We'll assess the work environment and make sure conditions are safe before starting. We've worked in warehouses, parking garages, enclosed courtyards, and more.
Do you bring your own power? +
Yes — our rig is self-contained. We don't need to plug into your power supply. We can work at locations without access to electricity.
Do you offer free estimates for mobile visits? +
Yes. Call or fill out the form and describe what you've got. We'll give you an estimate before we commit to the visit.
