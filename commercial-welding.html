Common Questions

Commercial Welding FAQ

Can you work after hours to avoid disrupting our business? +
Yes. We're flexible. Early mornings, evenings, weekends — we coordinate around your schedule so repairs don't shut down operations.
Do you handle ongoing work for property managers? +
Absolutely. Many of our commercial clients have us on standing call for their portfolios. We become the welder on your vendor list — consistent, responsive, and already familiar with your properties.
Do you carry liability insurance and are you licensed? +
Yes to both. We can provide certificates of insurance and licensing documentation as required for your records or your client's requirements.
Can you weld on-site at a commercial building? +
Yes — our mobile rig is built for commercial work. We bring everything we need and work on your site without requiring you to transport anything.
Do ADA handrails require permits? +
It depends on the scope and jurisdiction. We'll talk through requirements with you before the job and can help coordinate what's needed for permit-required work.
Do you offer free estimates for commercial work? +
Always. For larger commercial scopes, we'll do a proper on-site assessment before quoting so you get an accurate number — not a guess.
